Pruney Fingers: An Old Tale of Us

Keywords: ethical design, affective interaction, tangible interface, psychological first aid (PFA), sensory assistive technology, bodily playfulness, sensory XR

Related Projects: [You Will Know Me] [Wish You Were Here XR]

Question

Access to information of my body, denied. Is representation of female body in EDSA contents injurious to children's well being?

The Problem

Repeated ban on access to data due to gender bias and its implications: right to access data VS child protection.

The Solution

Multi-sensory, transmedia storytelling for public awareness and meaningful debate on gender fairness.

Methodology

Games, especially struggle game, as agency of art for better understanding and empathy in first-person, embodied play interaction.
