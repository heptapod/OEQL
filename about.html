More about me

I'm a slow but steady runner who loves fartlek LSD, running outside seeing, smelling, touching, and listening to nature. I have finished 5km, 10km, and half(21.1km) marathon races, preparing myself for my very first full(42.2km) marathon coming in January 2026. I can't wait to run enjoying the scenery of Ishigaki Island's sea.

I'm also a licensed free diver who can hold my breath for a maximum of 2 minutes and 45 seconds underwater, and dive to a depth of 11 meters. As the depth increases, pressure intensifies and fear naturally grows. Overcoming it and reaching the bottom, then looking up at the shining light filtering through the water—that is the best moment.

I love reading, and I've been a bookworm since childhood, even reading while walking. If I wasn't reading, I was building imaginary cities with my Lego blocks. As an adult, my favorite places to read and imagine are libraries and under trees. Most recently, I have been reading this paper.
