Wish You Were Here XR: Invisible Emergencies

Keywords: ethical design, affective interaction, tangible interface, psychological first aid (PFA), sensory assistive technology, bodily playfulness, sensory XR

Related Projects: [You Will Know Me] [Wish You Were Here VR]

Problem: Invisible Emergencies

This project started as an ethical consideration and solution to the problem discovered during play test sessions in participatory design of You Will Know Me project.

In pre-play test survey (n=37), significant number of participants shared their concerns with neurodiversity.

Solution: Wish You Were Here, an Illusion of Safety

1. Trauma-Informed and Neurodiversity-Informed Design for Psychological First Aid (PFA)

Multi-sensory affective interaction through visual, tactile, and olfactory feedback. Audio is intentionally excluded to support a sense of safety.

2. Somatic Design and Design Justice

"Nothing for us, nothing without us" — This principle guides our participatory design approach, ensuring that individuals with lived experience of trauma and neurodiversity are centered throughout the design process.
